Types of Trees (all trees are trimmed yearly)

Fraser Fir
The Farm features an abundance of Fraser Firs. Average mature size of Fraser Fir on our farm
is approximately up to 11 feet tall. Frasers are known and valued for their excellent needle retention,
beautiful silver-green color, and fresh aroma.

Blue Spruce & White Pine
Small quantities of pre-cut Colorado Blue Spruce and White Pine are also available.
