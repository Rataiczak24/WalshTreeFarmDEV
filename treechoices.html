Types of Trees (all trees are trimmed yearly)

Fraser Fir
The Farm features an abundance of Fraser Firs. Average mature size of Fraser Fir on our farm is approximately up to 11 feet tall. Frasers are known and valued for their excellent needle retention, beautiful silver-green color, and fresh aroma.

White Pine & Blue Spruce
Small quantities of pre-cut White Pine and Colorado Blue Spruce are also available.
