History of the Farm

Marlin Walsh began planting trees on 104 acres of land north of Newark in 1958. Little did he know what memories and fun times it would bring for generations of families to come. He is now with the Lord. His wife Carolyn, their four children, spouses, 15 grandchildren, and 17 great grandchildren have kept the farm going, along with the memories he left behind. The Farm has been family owned and growing trees since it's beginning. Come join us in the country and pick out a beautiful Christmas tree!
