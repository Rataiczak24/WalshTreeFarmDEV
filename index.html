-History of the Farm----------------------------------------------------------

Marlin Walsh began planting trees on 104 acres of land north of Newark
back in 1958. Little did he know what memories and fun times it would bring
for generations of families to come. He is now with the Lord, and his wife,
Carolyn, their four children and spouses, 15 grandchildren, and 10 great
grandchildren (and counting) have kept the farm going, along with all of
the memories he left behind. The Farm has been family owned and growing
trees since it's beginning.
